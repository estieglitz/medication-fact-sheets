Medication Fact Sheet Packet Builder

Create one printable PDF packet from APHON medication fact sheets by entering medications, optionally adding a protocol preset, and choosing English or Spanish.

Protocols are editable in protocols.json. Use this section after the medication box if you want to auto-fill a predefined bundle.
